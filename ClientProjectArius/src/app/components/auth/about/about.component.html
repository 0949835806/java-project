Important milestones

  • 1996: Established the first Super shop store located on a busy street of Hanoi capital at 194 Le Duan.
  • 2006: With 10 years of experience in retail,Super shop developed strongly and continuously opened many new branches.
  • 2016: Celebrating 20 years of operation in the field of retail technology products, Super shop has affirmed a solid foothold in the market as well as in the subconscious of consumers.
  • 2020: Super shop is proud to become the official authorized retailer of Apple in Vietnam. Genuine Apple VN/A products distributed by Apple Vietnam are sold at Super shop system with the best market price and prestigious warranty.

Vision and mission

Over the years, we have constantly improved our services at branches and supported customers through online channels. Super shop is committed to bringing quality products and prestigious warranty, ready to support customers in the fastest time. In the future, Super shop will continue to expand its branch system, aiming to be present in 63 provinces and cities nationwide. At the same time, improve service quality, limit risks, listen and absorb customers' suggestions to bring the best experience when shopping at Super shop. Finally, Super shop hopes to become a pioneer in bringing the latest technology products to users as soon as possible, creating a modern life where technology connects people, technology serves people.
